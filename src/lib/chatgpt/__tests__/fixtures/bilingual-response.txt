=== PRONUNCIATION NOTES ===
Word: Ram — RAAM — long "aa" vowel; do not shorten to "rum"
Word: kripa — KREE-paa — long final vowel; do not clip the "aa"
Word: daya — DAA-yaa — both vowels long; gentle flow
Word: Siya — SI-yaa — stress first syllable; long trailing "aa"
Word: apar — uh-PAAR — stress second syllable; rhymes with "par"
Word: vishwaas — vish-WAAS — long final vowel; aspirated "v"
