=== SONG TITLE ===
Ram Ki Kripa Endures

=== LYRICS ===
[Verse 1]
When darkness surrounds me and I cannot see
Ram's mercy reminds me that I am set free
The weight of the sorrow, the pain of the night
Is lifted by Ram Nam, the true morning light

[Chorus]
Ram ki kripa hai apar, apar
Ram ki daya kabhi na ho khatam
From the rising of the sun to where it sets at night
Ram ki kripa jagata ujiyaar

=== STYLE NOTES ===
Bhajan ballad style, 68 BPM, key of Sa/D major, harmonium and sarangi.

=== VOCAL GUIDANCE ===
Gentle phrasing throughout. Hold "kripa" in the chorus for emotional weight.

=== SUNO STYLE PROMPT ===
devotional bhajan ballad, harmonium and sarangi, 68 BPM, Sa/D major, tender female vocals, intimate and reflective

=== BACKGROUND ===
Based on the bhakti teaching that Ram's mercy (kripa) endures eternally, affirming the unfailing grace of the divine in the Vaishnava tradition.
